Tripod Support

How to Play

Tripod is a daily word puzzle where three words connect in a triangle. Fill in the letters to solve all three words — they share letters at the corners!

Need Help?

FAQ

When do new puzzles come out?

A new puzzle is available every day at midnight.

How do hints work?

Tap the hint button to reveal a letter. Use sparingly!
