How to Play

Objective

Fill in the triangle with three words that share a common category.

The Triangle

Each puzzle has three words that form a triangle:

  • Left edge — Word 1 (reads bottom to top)
  • Right edge — Word 2 (reads top to bottom)
  • Bottom row — Word 3 (reads left to right)

Shared Letters

The three words connect at the triangle's corners:

  • Top corner — Last letter of Word 1 = First letter of Word 2
  • Bottom-left — First letter of Word 1 = First letter of Word 3
  • Bottom-right — Last letter of Word 2 = Last letter of Word 3

Feedback Colors

Green — Letter is in the correct position
Yellow — Valid word, but in the wrong position
Red + shake — No correct letters
